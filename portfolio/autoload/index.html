Load Planning & Optimization Softwares

During my tenure in 2010, I led the software development department and designed optimization algorithms for vehicle allocation and container load planning. Our flagship products, AutoLoad and AutoDispatch, were developed in C# and C. AutoLoad is a sophisticated load planning and optimization software that utilizes a two-stage loading optimization technology to efficiently organize loads on trucks, containers, cartons, and pallets. AutoDispatch is an advanced vehicle routing and delivery scheduling optimization software that streamlines the dispatch process for enhanced operational efficiency.

2010年，我擔任軟體開發部門主管，負責設計配車與裝箱規劃的最佳化運算演算法。我們的旗艦產品AutoLoad與AutoDispatch採用C#和C語言開發。AutoLoad是一款先進的裝載規劃與最佳化軟體，利用兩階段裝載最佳化技術，高效組織貨物於卡車、貨櫃、紙箱和棧板上。AutoDispatch是一款高級的車輛路徑和送貨排程最佳化軟體，優化派遣過程，提升運營效率。
