Overview of Vortex Intelligent Data Sharing Platform - 20160217

At the time, our company had just acquired Prismtech, a software company based in the UK. The entire company had limited knowledge about the products and DDS (Data Distribution Service) of this subsidiary. As a pioneer, I took the initiative to study the products of this company and provided a brief introduction to DDS within our department, facilitating a quick understanding for our colleagues to get started.
