Smart House Demo Kit

Under my leadership, our team demonstrated the industrial scalability of our M2M router tailored for industrial applications by developing an innovative prototype model house. This pioneering project leveraged the synergy of MQTT and Modbus technologies to enable a web-based control of the model house via an iOS app, a novelty at that time. Successfully navigating through the App Store's rigorous review, the app significantly elevated our company's visibility and sparked widespread interest at various exhibitions.

在我的領導下，我們的團隊透過開發創新的原型模型屋，展示了我們針對工業應用設計的 M2M 路由器的產業擴展能力。這一開創性項目結合了 MQTT 和 Modbus 技術，實現了透過 iOS 網頁應用對模型屋的遠程控制，當時這樣的創新尚屬罕見。App成功上架至 App Store ，顯著提高了公司在多次展覽中的曝光度。
Role: Development Team Leader
