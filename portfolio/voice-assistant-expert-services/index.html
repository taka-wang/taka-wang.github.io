Voice Assistant Expert Services - 20181030

On October 30, 2018, in my role as Senior Product Manager, I began exploring various research topics, including baby monitors and facial recognition access control systems. It was during this time that I came up with the idea for a voice assistant computer fan. I presented this concept to the management team, explaining the complexities of integrating Google Assistant Actions and Alexa Custom Skills. I also proposed incorporating IFTTT integration. The RGB fan is consistent with our parent company's product line, representing a unique blend of software and hardware concepts.

2018年10月30日，作為高級產品經理，我開始探索各種研究課題，其中包括寶寶監視器和人臉辨識門禁系統。 正是在這段時間裡，我萌生了語音助手風扇的想法。 我向管理團隊介紹了這個概念，解釋了整合Google智能助理操作和亞馬遜 Alexa 自訂技能的複雜性。我還提出了加入 IFTTT 整合。 這種 RGB 風扇與我們母公司的產品線保持一致，代表了軟體和硬體概念的獨特融合。
Role: Senior Product Manager
